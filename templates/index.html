FAQ
Is it free to use?+
Yes — completely free, forever. No account, no subscription, no hidden limits on daily downloads.
Do you store my downloaded files?+
No. Files are deleted from our server automatically within 10 minutes of generation.
What formats and quality are available?+
Videos are saved as MP4 (up to 1080p HD). Audio is delivered as MP3 at 192 kbps. Photos arrive as JPEG at the platform's original resolution.
Can I download from private accounts?+
No. Only publicly accessible content can be fetched. If a post requires login to view, we cannot access it either.
Is this legal?+
Saving public content for personal, offline use is generally permitted. Copyright remains with the original creator — please don't redistribute content without permission.
